Portal Traits

Baby Portal

Colour: bright pink;

Danger level: 0;

What it does: takes you out of Invisible, Fire, and Ocean portals

Invisible Portal

Colour: clear;

Danger level: 2;

What it does: makes your car invisible

Dead Portal

Colour: grey;

Danger level: 0;

What it does: takes you out of Invisible, Fire, and Ocean portals

Fire Portal

Colour: orange;

Danger level: 5;

What it does: brings your car to the Fire World

Water Portal

Colour: bright blue;

Danger level: 3;

What it does: brings you to the Ocean World

Worlds

Fire World

This is a place full of fire. Depending on which level you're on, this can be the most dangerous one!

Ocean World

This is a place full of water, and friendly fish. Depending on which level you're on, the fish give you an extra live if you come directly from the Fire World!

The Mission

Sad face means you lost, happy face means you won.

W, A, S, and D control the red car.

I, J, K, and L control the blue car, but the blue car is only gained by pressing the upgrade button.

Escape brings you out of the full screen.

You have to select your level before you can start playing.

If Sactintra gets you, (after upgrading) you have to start over, and the code for upgrading changes.
